App Guide
Get the most out of MindFile.
iPhone · Add to Home Screen
1
Open in SafariMust be Safari — Chrome and Firefox don't support home screen install on iOS.
2
Tap the Share iconThe box with an arrow pointing up — at the bottom of Safari.
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen"Scroll down in the share sheet until you see it, then tap Add.
4
Open from your home screenIt launches full-screen like a native app, no browser chrome.
Navigation
🏠
Home icon — your dashboard with recent notes and category overview.
✦
Star icon — All Notes view, organised by category. Tap a category to expand it.
＋
Plus button — add a new note. Assign a category, add tags, or pin it.
🔍
Search — tap the magnifying glass to search across all your notes instantly.
Notes
✏️
Edit a note — tap any note to open the edit sheet. Change text, category, tags, or mark it done.
📌
Pin a note — tap the 📌 in the compose or edit sheet to pin it to the top of its category.
🏷️
Tags — type a tag in the Tags field and press space or return to add it. Filter by tag in All Notes.
📂
Categories — tap to expand. Tap again while open to edit name or colour.
Backup & Restore
💾
Full Backup — Settings → Backup & Restore → Full Backup. Saves everything as a .json file. Use this to migrate to a new version.
📥
Restore — tap Import Backup and choose your saved .json file. Notes are merged, nothing is deleted.
